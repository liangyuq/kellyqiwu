Artifact 1: AI Evolution Timeline

Introduction: This artifact is a visual timeline that highlights key moments in the history of artificial intelligence, from its origin in 1956 to the rise of ChatGPT in 2022.

Description: The timeline summarizes nine major milestones that shaped AI development, including early breakthroughs, AI winters, deep learning successes, and landmark AI victories in games and cloud adoption.

Objective: To communicate the historical evolution of AI in a clear, simple, and visual way for audiences unfamiliar with technical details. This piece is designed as an educational resource to spark interest and awareness of how AI has grown.

Process: I researched major AI events and breakthroughs, wrote simplified descriptions using plain language, and designed a clean, scroll-friendly timeline using Canva. I organized events chronologically and color-coded categories to improve readability.

Tools/Technologies Used: Canva for visual design, ChatGPT for drafting timeline descriptions, and image optimization tools to prepare the visual for web use.

Value Proposition: This visual helps simplify a complex topic and engages a broad audience through design. It’s useful for educators, students, and professionals seeking a quick understanding of AI’s evolution. It also shows my ability to translate technical content into engaging visuals, a key skill in instructional design and communication.

References: Timeline based on information from Stanford AI Index, OpenAI blog, DeepMind articles, and other public sources on key AI milestones.
